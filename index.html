About the Congress

The IEEE Delhi Section Student Young Professionals Women in Engineering Life Member Congress (DSSYWLC) is a flagship event aimed at bringing together the brightest minds across various domains of technology and leadership.

Our goal is to foster innovation, facilitate networking, and provide a platform for professional growth through workshops, keynote sessions, and collaborative projects.
